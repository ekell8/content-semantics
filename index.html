Beetroot (Beta vulgaris)

  1. Common varieties
  2. Nutritional information
  3. Tags

55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest the root.

The usually deep purple roots of the beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vegatable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinager, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegatable.

In fact, Jim Halpert once said this about beets, "Fact, bears eat beets. Bears, beets, Battlestar Galactica."
